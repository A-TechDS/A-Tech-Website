How we Work

Working with an agency can be daunting. We feel your pain.

Simplified at best.

After many coffees and multiple packets of scotch fingers, we have finally discovered the ultimate process to ensure the best product is delivered every time. We call it "listening to the client".

At the end of the day, this is your project. It's not like we can tell you how to raise your child right? If you want your child to learn the piano then we must respect that decision even though we think your child should learn to play the guitar instead. This is the principal on which we consult here at A-Tech. We work with our clients to first establish specific details such as color, layout, assets ( images video etc) and in some cases music. It is on these foundations our team work their magic to produce stunning results.

Quality is our middle name.

Like well aged wine, your product should also age well with time. People buy NIKE (other brands are available) sneakers because they last 10x longer than sneakers bought from a $2 shop. Quality always speaks for itself and that's what we like to think our clients will feel after seeing their dream transform into reality. Being in business is a journey on it's own and we would be honored to join you on this wonderful path to success. To summarize in plain english. Our process looks something like this:

  • 30 min consultation
  • Project proposal draft
  • Prototype UX designs
  • Alpha product
  • Final feedback
  • Product launch
  • SEO/marketing
  • Huge smiles
