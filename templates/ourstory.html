From Kitchens to Codes

Being a 90s kid I grew up with technology. But as they say if you are used to one thing you never take it seriously until you leave it for a while. That's exactly what happened the day I quit my job as an apprentice chef for one of the best universities in Auckland New Zealand. This sudden feeling of not reaching my true potential dawned on me to the point where I went back to where I felt most at home.. Tech.

Getting back into technology, in particular hardware seemed almost impossible now unless you're Bill Gates himself. Then the lightbulb turned on, development. Web, Software, Game, Animation development is the answer. That's when my curiosity really arose. I kept heading deeper into the rabbit hole that is programming and found myself emerging with these new superhuman like powers.

Suddenly a new world of possiblilities to conquer seemed possible.As Uncle Ben says "with great power, comes great responsibility". Okay that's maybe a bit too dramatised but you get the point I'm trying to make here. If you're not happy doing what you do everyday, then do something that makes you happy everyday.
