Python Object Oriented Job Assistant (P.O.O.J.A)

Our own JARVIS. Minus the Tony Stark of course

A project undertaken by A-tech. This AI personal assistant is made with Python and can search the internet for absolutely anything you desire! It even searches for answers through Wikipedia, answers computational and mathematical questions thanks to Wolfram Alpha, play music from YouTube without lifting your mouse! We personally use POOJA to synchronize our calendars within our business (She can even tell us when our next meeting is!), share inspirational quotes through the power of API's and the applications for this technology are never ending. If you ever wanted to have your own JARVIS then 2022 is the year for you!


Madeleine Pizzuti

Website coming soon!


Madeleine began her writing career at the age of sixty-four when she published her first book - TELL ME A STORY - detailing the many adventures of her happy childhood spent growing up in the Fenland district of England. The inspiration for her second book - BEAR - came from the teddybear belonging to her eldest grandson. It is a delightful story of family life, as told through the eyes of the teddybear. Madeleine's latest publication - UNDER THE WEEPING WILLOW - is the tale of an English village community and the drama, gossip and romance that form part of village life.
