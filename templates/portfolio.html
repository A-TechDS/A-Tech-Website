Madeleine began her writing career at the age of sixty-four when she published her first book - TELL ME A STORY - detailing the many adventures of her happy childhood spent growing up in the Fenland district of England. The inspiration for her second book - BEAR - came from the teddybear belonging to her eldest grandson. It is a delightful story of family life, as told through the eyes of the teddybear. Madeleine's latest publication - UNDER THE WEEPING WILLOW - is the tale of an English village community and the drama, gossip and romance that form part of village life.
